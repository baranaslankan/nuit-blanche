The Project
The Eaton Centre is transformed as balloons are used to construct a psychedelic landscape of swaying tunnels and extensions.

A maze-like fort of balloons is formed; its airy terrain is a tangle of countless interior chambers. People are invited to add to the structure, enter into it or just explore its various wings.
